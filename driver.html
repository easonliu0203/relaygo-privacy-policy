RELAY GO 司機端隱私權政策

Driver Privacy Policy

1. 導言

歡迎您申請加入 RELAY GO 司機夥伴（以下簡稱「本平台」）。我們致力於保護您的個人隱私權。本隱私權政策旨在說明當您註冊及使用 RELAY GO 司機端 App 及其相關服務（以下簡稱「本服務」）時，我們如何收集、使用、處理及保護您的個人資訊。

在您註冊成為司機夥伴前，請務必詳細閱讀並同意本政策。

2. 我們收集的資訊

為遵守台灣交通法規對包車司機資格的規範，並為您提供接單及帳務服務，我們需要您提供以下「必填」資訊：

A. 身份驗證與合法資格審核資料

重要說明：以下資料用於確保您符合台灣法律對包車司機的資格要求，我們將採取最高級別的安全措施保護這些敏感資料。

  • 姓名、身分證字號及影像：用於核實您的真實身份。
  • 職業駕照影像：用於確認您具備合法的駕駛資格。
  • 良民證（警察刑事紀錄證明書）：依據法規要求，確認您符合職業駕駛的無犯罪紀錄標準。
  • 無肇事紀錄（駕駛執照經歷證明書）：用於確認您的駕駛安全紀錄。
  • 車輛資料：車輛行照、汽車保險（強制險及乘客責任險）證明，以確保車輛符合營運標準及乘客安全保障。
  • 靠行公司資訊（如適用）：包含公司名稱與統一編號，用於確認車輛歸屬及營運合法性。

B. 帳務與聯絡資料

  • 銀行收款帳戶：包含銀行名稱、分行代碼及帳號，僅用於向您轉帳支付您的服務款項（車資）。
  • 電話號碼：用於帳號驗證、接收訂單通知、乘客聯繫及平台重要聯絡。
  • 電子信箱：用於帳號管理、接收帳務報表及重要通知。
  • 住址：用於文件寄送及確認營運區域。

C. 自動收集的資訊（使用本平台APP期間）

  • 精確地理位置：我們會在您上線接單期間，持續收集您的精確地理位置，用於派發訂單、行程導航、計算費用及確保行程安全。
  • 行程與交易資訊：您完成的訂單記錄、行程時間、路線、收款金額及乘客評價。
  • App 使用資訊：您的裝置型號、作業系統、IP 位址及 App 操作日誌，用於優化服務及排除錯誤。

3. 資訊的使用目的

我們承諾僅將您的資訊用於以下特定目的：

A. 合法司機資格核對（主要目的）

  • 將您提供的身分證、駕照、良民證、無肇事紀錄等文件，用於內部審核及（必要時）提交給主管機關，以驗證您在台灣作為包車服務司機的合法性與合規性。

B. 轉帳給司機（主要目的）

  • 使用您提供的收款帳戶資訊，定期將您應得的車資結算並轉帳給您。
  • 製作並寄送帳務報表或年度扣繳憑單。

C. 營運與派單

  • 根據您的即時位置，向您派發最合適的乘客訂單。
  • 向乘客顯示您的姓名、車輛資訊（車型、車號）及預計抵達時間。

D. 安全與客服

  • 在行程中監控位置以保障司機與乘客安全。
  • 處理乘客的客訴或行程爭議。
  • 回應您的客服請求。

4. 資訊的分享與揭露

我們對您的敏感資料（特別是身份文件）採取最高級別的保護，僅在必要時分享：

  • 分享給乘客：當您接單後，乘客將能看到您的姓名（或暱稱）、駕駛照片、車輛型號及車牌號碼，以便乘客辨識及安心上車。您的電話號碼將被遮罩，乘客僅能透過 App 內建通訊功能聯繫您。
  • 主管機關要求：依據台灣法律或交通主管機關（如交通部、監理所）的合法要求，我們可能需要提供您的身份及營運資料以供查核。
  • 帳務處理：您的收款帳戶資訊僅會分享給執行轉帳作業的金融機構（銀行）。

5. 資料安全與儲存

我們理解司機身份文件的敏感性。

A. 存取限制

您所提供的所有身份驗證文件（身分證、駕照、良民證等）將被儲存在受高度安全管制的伺服器（如 Firebase Storage / Supabase Storage）中，並採用嚴格的存取控制，僅有經授權的營運審核人員才能在必要時（例如首次審核或年度複查）存取。

B. 加密保護

所有資料在傳輸及儲存過程中均使用 SSL 及伺服器端加密技術保護。

6. 您的權利

  • 存取與修改：您可以隨時登入 App 查看您的個人檔案、收款帳戶及車輛資訊，並可請求修改錯誤的資料。
  • 刪除帳號（結束合作）：當您決定不再與本平台合作時，您可以透過 App 內的「個人檔案」→「最下面」→「刪除帳號」功能請求刪除您的帳號。依據法規，我們可能需要在特定期限內（例如帳務或稅務規定）保留您的部分交易及身份資料備查，待期限屆滿後將予以刪除。

7. 隱私權政策變更

我們可能隨時修訂本政策。若有重大變更，我們將透過 App 推播通知或您註冊的電子信箱通知您。

8. 聯絡我們

若您對本隱私權政策或您的資料處理有任何疑問，請透過以下方式與我們聯繫：

1. Introduction

Welcome to apply to become a RELAY GO driver partner (hereinafter referred to as "the Platform"). We are committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and safeguard your personal information when you register and use the RELAY GO Driver App and related services (hereinafter referred to as "the Service").

Please read and agree to this policy before registering as a driver partner.

2. Information We Collect

To comply with Taiwan's traffic regulations regarding charter driver qualifications and to provide you with order acceptance and accounting services, we require the following mandatory information:

A. Identity Verification and Legal Qualification Review Data

Important Notice: The following data is used to ensure you meet Taiwan's legal requirements for charter drivers. We will employ the highest level of security measures to protect this sensitive data.

  • Name, ID Number, and ID Image: Used to verify your real identity.
  • Professional Driver's License Image: Used to confirm you have legal driving qualifications.
  • Police Criminal Record Certificate: As required by regulations, to confirm you meet the no-criminal-record standard for professional drivers.
  • No-Accident Record (Driving License History Certificate): Used to confirm your driving safety record.
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle registration, automobile insurance (compulsory insurance and passenger liability insurance) certificates to ensure the vehicle meets operational standards and passenger safety guarantees.
  • Affiliated Company Information (if applicable): Including company name and unified business number, used to confirm vehicle ownership and operational legality.

B. Accounting and Contact Information

  • Bank Account for Payment: Including bank name, branch code, and account number, used only to transfer your service payments (fares).
  • Phone Number: Used for account verification, receiving order notifications, passenger contact, and important platform communications.
  • Email Address: Used for account management, receiving accounting reports, and important notifications.
  • Address: Used for document delivery and confirming operational areas.

C. Automatically Collected Information (During Platform App Use)

  • Precise Geographic Location: We continuously collect your precise geographic location during your online order acceptance period for order dispatch, trip navigation, fee calculation, and ensuring trip safety.
  • Trip and Transaction Information: Your completed order records, trip times, routes, payment amounts, and passenger ratings.
  • App Usage Information: Your device model, operating system, IP address, and App operation logs for service optimization and troubleshooting.

3. Purpose of Information Use

We commit to using your information only for the following specific purposes:

A. Legal Driver Qualification Verification (Primary Purpose)

  • Use the ID, driver's license, police certificate, no-accident record, and other documents you provide for internal review and (when necessary) submission to competent authorities to verify your legality and compliance as a charter service driver in Taiwan.

B. Transfer Payments to Drivers (Primary Purpose)

  • Use your provided payment account information to regularly settle and transfer your earned fares to you.
  • Prepare and send accounting reports or annual withholding certificates.

C. Operations and Order Dispatch

  • Based on your real-time location, dispatch the most suitable passenger orders to you.
  • Display your name, vehicle information (vehicle type, license plate), and estimated arrival time to passengers.

D. Safety and Customer Service

  • Monitor location during trips to ensure driver and passenger safety.
  • Handle passenger complaints or trip disputes.
  • Respond to your customer service requests.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We employ the highest level of protection for your sensitive data (especially identity documents) and only share when necessary:

  • Shared with Passengers: After you accept an order, passengers will be able to see your name (or nickname), driver photo, vehicle model, and license plate number so passengers can identify and board with confidence. Your phone number will be masked, and passengers can only contact you through the App's built-in communication features.
  • Competent Authority Requirements: In accordance with Taiwan law or lawful requests from transportation authorities (such as the Ministry of Transportation, Motor Vehicle Offices), we may need to provide your identity and operational data for inspection.
  • Accounting Processing: Your payment account information will only be shared with financial institutions (banks) executing transfer operations.

5. Data Security and Storage

We understand the sensitivity of driver identity documents.

A. Access Restrictions

All identity verification documents you provide (ID, driver's license, police certificate, etc.) will be stored on highly secure servers (such as Firebase Storage / Supabase Storage) with strict access controls. Only authorized operational review personnel can access them when necessary (such as initial review or annual re-examination).

B. Encryption Protection

All data is protected using SSL and server-side encryption technology during transmission and storage.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and Modification: You can log into the App at any time to view your personal profile, payment account, and vehicle information, and request correction of incorrect data.
  • Account Deletion (End Cooperation): When you decide to no longer cooperate with the Platform, you can request deletion of your account through the App's "Profile" → "Bottom" → "Delete Account" feature. According to regulations, we may need to retain some of your transaction and identity data for a specific period (such as accounting or tax requirements) for inspection, and will delete it after the period expires.

7. Privacy Policy Changes

We may revise this policy at any time. In the event of significant changes, we will notify you via App push notifications or the email address you registered.
